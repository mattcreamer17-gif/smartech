Smartech360 delivers Odoo implementations for small businesses across South Florida. The homepage leads with "All-in-One Tech. Real-Time Decisions. The tension

The AI sections on your site lean on Odoo's built-in features: document OCR, AI chatbots, AI-generated content. Any prospect who checks notices. A methodology that decides, per workflow, whether the right answer is Odoo, custom code, a rule engine, a chatbot, or a human sits one layer above the tools and makes the AI story defensible.

A practical way to think about any Smartech360 client

60%

Plain software

Import the data. Configure the modules. Wire the reports. Odoo does this well and so does your team.

30%

Rule-based logic

Approval flows, pricing rules, inventory thresholds. A short, explicit rule engine beats a chatbot every time.

10%

Real AI

Spanish-first customer conversations. Inconsistent invoice extraction. Summarizing a sales call in plain language.

That ten percent is where a packaged Smartech360 AI SKU earns its price. The other ninety percent earns its price by not pretending to be AI.

The methodology is published

ICM, the Interpretable Context Methodology, organizes each engagement's context (client policies, data, workflows, identity) as a layered filesystem that humans and agents can both read. Submitted to ACM TiiS. The repo is MIT licensed. github.com/RinDig/Interpretable-Context-Methodology-ICM-

For clients with real governance exposure (healthcare, finance, regulated SMB), we also ship the Ethics Engine, a psychometric assessment tool for evaluating ideological and moral patterns in LLMs. Paper: arxiv.org/abs/2510.11742. Repo: github.com/RinDig/AuditEngine.
